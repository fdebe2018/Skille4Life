About

Founded in 1995, Skills 4 Life was first designed to inform business and technology students on how to correctly format their portfolios. Over the years as the amount of viewers progressed and the content did as well. Skills 4 Life was not only focused on portfolios but also thought to help with job interviews and job applications, teaching young and old the appropriate way to get a job.

Our Values

MISSION: Our mission is to ensure that everyone knows the basic information required to get the job suited for them.

VISION: Eventually, we would like to see that everyone has the tools and knowledge to be successful, so that unemployment decreases and employment increases.
